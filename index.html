This graph shows the top 5 countries in the world for the most womens Olympic medals. You will notice that the USA and Canada lead the world for the most womens medals, both countries also lead in overall medals. An interesting fact however is the third country in womens medals, Germany, they are actually eighth in the world for overall medals.

Break Down of Type of Medals Won by Women Olypians in Each Country

In breaking down the types of medals won by women in each country we see that Canada dominates the world in most gold medals, the USA on the other hand has primarily won silver. The USSR is slightly high in gold medals also while Germany is tied for equal amounts gold and silver medals and Sweden for silver and bronze.

Ratio of Medal Winners by Gender in the Top 5 Countries

These graphs show the ratio of mens medalists vs womans medalists in each country. You will notice that Canada and The USA both have about one third of women vs two thirds of men winning medals. In Germany however nearly half of their medal winners are women. Sweden and the USSR are sitting at about a quarter of their medals being won by women athletes.
